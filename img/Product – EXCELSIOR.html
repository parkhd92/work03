BOLT

As “EXCELSIOR” brand launch, Bolt Collection is the first collection of “EXCELSIOR” to the market. It is the entry line of brand and shows clearly brand concept. The products in Bolt Collection is the basic items, and has the roughness with masculinity as well as feeling of bold associated with its pronunciation, which is aligned with the direction we pursue.
Having classic canvas upper and attractive rugged outsole design, Bolt Collection has a outsole constructed with deeply impressive Foxing tape(mid-sole).
This is the icon, which is expressing well EXCELSIOR concept “Industrial Classic” and is inspired by a part of tire closely related with the development of rubber industry.
In order to apply this to the shoes outsole, we have been collaborating with the best craftsmen in our country who have working on Vulcanized Shoes for decades, and have developed the unprecedented Foxing-tape through a lot trial and error and finally applied this to Bolt Collection.
In addition, after-processing treatment with enamel onto the outsole is the process which makes it fully felt the Craftsmanship since this treatment has been done by expert master craftsmen while they paint and dry them one by one. By doing this, outsole itself can be clearly exposed with quiet gloss and have advantage for preventing various contamination and discoloration.
Bolt collection consists of low cut & hi cut. Low one is available on 65$ and Hi cut is on 69$.
